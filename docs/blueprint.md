# **App Name**: MindMirror+

## Core Features:

- AI Avatar Reflection: AI Avatar Reflection: Generates an AI avatar based on the user's input about their current emotional state. The avatar updates to reflect these changes, acting as a visual 'mirror' of the user's feelings. This tool decides what is most relevant.
- Gamified Growth Tracker: Gamified Growth Tracker: A 'Progress Garden' visually represents the user's emotional growth over time. Users earn badges for achieving emotional milestones, encouraging continued engagement.
- Safe Anonymous Space: Safe Anonymous Whisper Space: Provides a secure, anonymous space for users to share their thoughts and feelings without judgment, fostering a sense of community and belonging.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft Lavender (#D0B8FF) to evoke calmness and introspection, mirroring the app's focus on emotional well-being.
- Background color: Light Gray (#F5F5F5) to create a neutral and unobtrusive backdrop, ensuring the focus remains on the emotional content.
- Accent color: Gentle Blue (#A0D2EB) used for interactive elements and highlights, symbolizing trust and security in the anonymous space.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for a modern yet approachable feel, ensuring readability and a sense of warmth.
- Use gentle, minimalist icons that represent different emotions and milestones in the growth tracker. Icons should be consistent in style and complement the overall calming aesthetic.
- Employ a clean and intuitive layout with ample spacing to prevent overcrowding. Prioritize ease of navigation and a distraction-free experience, allowing users to focus on their emotional journey.
- Incorporate subtle, calming animations for transitions and feedback, enhancing the user experience without causing overwhelm. For instance, the 'Progress Garden' could feature gentle growth animations as milestones are achieved.
